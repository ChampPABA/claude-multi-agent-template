# PRD: Cacao Craft Landing Page

## Project Overview

**Product Name:** Cacao Craft
**Type:** E-commerce Landing Page
**Industry:** Premium Cacao Products / Artisan Beverages

---

## Business Description

Cacao Craft is a premium cacao café and product retailer specializing in high-quality, organic cacao products. Similar to a specialty coffee shop, but focused entirely on cacao - from fresh cacao drinks to artisan chocolate products.

**Core Values:**
- Organic, pesticide-free farming
- Health-conscious products
- Artisan craftsmanship
- Farm-to-cup quality

**Product Lines:**
1. **Cacao Beverages** - Hot cacao, iced cacao, specialty drinks
2. **Cacao Mass** - Pure cacao paste for cooking/baking
3. **Artisan Chocolate** - Bean-to-bar chocolate, truffles, bonbons
4. **Cacao Nibs & Powder** - Raw ingredients
5. **Gift Sets** - Curated cacao product collections

---

## Target Audience

### Primary Audience
- **Age:** 25-45 years old
- **Demographics:** Urban professionals, health-conscious consumers
- **Psychographics:**
  - Value organic/sustainable products
  - Interested in health benefits
  - Appreciate artisan craftsmanship
  - Willing to pay premium for quality

### Secondary Audience
- Foodies and culinary enthusiasts
- Gift shoppers
- Wellness community

**What users should feel:**
1. **Trust** - Organic, safe, high-quality
2. **Health** - Nutritious, beneficial
3. **Warmth** - Welcoming, like a café
4. **Discovery** - Learn about cacao benefits

## Key Features Needed

**Landing Page Sections:**
1. Hero - Showcase premium cacao products visually
2. About - Story of organic farming, health benefits
3. Products - Visual gallery with categories
4. Benefits - Why cacao? Health advantages
5. Process - Farm-to-cup journey
6. Testimonials - Customer reviews
7. Call-to-Action - Visit café, shop online

---

## Visual Direction Preferences

**Desired Aesthetic:**
- Natural, earthy tones (browns, greens, creams)
- Warm and inviting (like a café atmosphere)
- Product photography-forward (high-quality images)
- Clean but not sterile
- Premium but approachable

**Avoid:**
- Too corporate/formal
- Too playful/childish
- Dark/gloomy
- Overly minimalist (need warmth)
